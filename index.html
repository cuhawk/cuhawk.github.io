Cyber Security (March 2021 - June 2022)

Started from TryHackMe, with the programming and basic web development and handling servers on AWS and GCP. Learnt the basics usage of tools and concepts like SQLi, XSS, CSRF, Basic Enumeration, Metasploit, Web Attacks, Burpsuite and other tools and techniques. Later switched to HackTheBox. The Easy, Medium, Hard and Insane Boxes took the difficulty to literally another level. Youtuber IppSec, John Hammond and 0xdf taught a lot. After a couple of months, I decided to take OSCP course from Offensive Security. They had changed the syllabus in 2020, so the content was very new, equally difficult too. Completed 70+ machines from the lab after the PDF and Videos. Took me 3 months for the lab only. Now since I clearly needed more experience, I switched back to doing the old machines on HackTheBox for a couple of months. Then gave the OSCP Exam. Completed 70 marks of machines which was exactly what was expected. But I never concentrated at Documentation, so I was declared Failed. I again rebounded to HaackTheBox, did a few more boxes. It had been over six months since I got involved in Cyber Security. I now took the OSEP course since OSCP is a beginner level course I wanted to try something really difficult and challenging.
